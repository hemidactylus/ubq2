About UBIQueue

UBQ, a system to display online the status of take-a-number systems: keep an eye on when it's going to be your turn while you run another errand - or simply enjoy your coffee!
A project imagined, planned and developed by salamandrina.net for Ordine degli Avvocati di Ivrea .
To contact the author, or have a look at the server-side source code, go to the project's github .
